The Space

A hilltop sanctuary for embodied living

Two minutes from downtown Mazunte. Seven from the beach. Entrance next to Falafel Loco, opposite the organic market.

Where daily morning practices and communal meals create a retreat-like space for reconnection.

Casa Yeshúa sits on a hilltop overlooking Mazunte, surrounded by forest. Part hostel, part yoga shala, part community kitchen — a place built for people who want to live intentionally, not just pass through.

The space runs on a simple rhythm: morning practice, shared meals, evening gathering. No agenda beyond presence. No prerequisites beyond showing up.

This is not a party hostel. It's a sober conscious space — a sanctuary for travelers, movers, musicians, and seekers who want depth over distraction.

Every day begins with breathwork, yoga, and ice bath. Every week builds toward the Sunday Full Experience — the heartbeat of Mazunte's rainy season. Six months of movement, music, and ritual, open to anyone who finds their way here.

Vegan and vegetarian food. Respect, presence, connection. Come as you are.

Sunday Full Experience

A sacred space to move, breathe, express and connect in community

Four experiences woven into one afternoon. Stay for all of it, or join the parts that call to you.

3:00 pm

Playtime

Ice Bath + Workshop

Connect to yourself and one another through interactive vocal and movement games. A playful and dynamic space to liberate your inner child and open to creativity. The ice bath wakes you up. The workshop keeps you there.

5:00 – 7:00 pm

Ecstatic Dance

Two hours of unstructured movement. No steps to learn, no way to get it wrong. No talking on the dance floor, no phones, no shoes. Just you, the music, and the rain on the roof.

with DJ Ur Magic

7:00 pm

Community Dinner

A shared meal of 5–7 vegetarian and vegan dishes, made with organic ingredients from local producers. Sit with people you just danced with. Eat slowly. Stay a while.

8:00 pm

Singing Circle

Close the evening with voice. Guided songs and harmonies in a circle — simple melodies that anyone can follow. Invite your family to share their voices and instruments. The day started with cold water and ends with warm voices.

guided by Félix

Dance in the rain, open your heart, feel alive.

Stay With Us

More than a Sunday

Casa Yeshúa is also a place to sleep, eat, and live. Stay for a night, a week, or the whole season.

Dorms + Camping

Affordable beds and camping spots on a hilltop with ocean views. Linen, mosquito nets, security lockers.

Ocean-View Yoga Shala

Daily morning rituals: breathwork, yoga, ice bath, breakfast. Move before the day begins.

Communal Kitchen + Bar

Shared kitchen, organic ingredients, community dinners. The bar is for evenings. No curfew, 24h security.

For Conscious Travelers

Yogis, artists, healers, seekers. Not a party hostel. Free WiFi, pets welcome.

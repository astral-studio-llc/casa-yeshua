The Journey

A sacred space to move, breathe, express and connect in community

Four experiences woven into one afternoon. Stay for all of it, or join the parts that call to you.

3:00 pm

Playtime

Ice Bath + Workshop

Connect to yourself and one another through interactive vocal and movement games. A playful and dynamic space to liberate your inner child and open to creativity.

5:00 – 7:00 pm

Ecstatic Dance

Two hours of unstructured movement. No steps to learn, no way to get it wrong. Just you, the music, and the rain.

with DJ Ur Magic

7:00 pm

Community Dinner

Nourishing vegetarian and vegan dishes. Sit with people you just danced with. Share a meal, share a moment.

8:00 – 8:30 pm

Singing Circle

Close the evening with voice. Guided songs and harmonies in a circle. No experience needed — just an open heart.

guided by Felix

Dance in the rain, open your heart, feel alive.
